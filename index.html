About Us

ActiveSG is a national movement for sport where all can experience the joy of living better through sport.

ActiveSG is a platform that will strengthen and extend the reach of our sporting ecosystem in Singapore.

Through ActiveSG programmes and activities for all ages and abilities, it aims to build communities, increase sports participation and ultimately encourage Singaporeans to live better through sport
